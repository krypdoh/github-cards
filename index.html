Open source Windows utilities & desktop tools

MLB-TCKR

Live baseball scores docked at the top of your desktop. Check scores, inning flow, and matchup details without opening a browser tab.

RainDelay

Take a break. Let it rain. Video-composited rain with screen-blend rendering over your blurred desktop, with ambient sound.

GhostDesktop

Now you see it, now you don't. Dynamically fades desktop icons based on focus or hover. Keep your desktop clean and minimal.

WinSifter

Stop living in a messy folder. Automatically sorts any folder into clean, categorized subfolders — in one click, with a live preview.

TCKR

Your stocks, always visible. A customizable, always-on-top stock and cryptocurrency ticker with real-time prices and LED effects.

DropStop

Temporarily pause Dropbox syncing with a single click or command. Set a timer, walk away, and it resumes automatically.

WinPath

A mouse-enabled terminal UI for managing Windows PATH environment variables. Click it. Type it. Either way, it just works.

UnzipadeeDownloads

Drop an archive in your Downloads folder and it's already extracted — automatically, silently, instantly.

SyncFreeze

Freeze background sync activity on demand to keep bandwidth and disk usage quiet while you focus, then resume when ready.
